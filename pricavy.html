Privacy Policy

Prompt Compass is a browser extension that runs locally in your browser to help organize, collapse, and label ChatGPT messages for your personal use.

Data Collection

This extension does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data, chat content, or usage analytics.

All labels, folders, and preferences are stored locally using your browserâ€™s localStorage or chrome.storage.local. These are not shared or uploaded anywhere.

Permissions

  • activeTab: Used to activate the extension only on the ChatGPT page.
  • storage: Used to save your local labels and folder settings in your browser.
  • Host permission: Applies only to https://chat.openai.com and https://chatgpt.com where the extension enhances the interface.

Third Parties

This extension does not share data with any third parties.

If this privacy policy changes in the future, it will be updated here before any new version is published.
